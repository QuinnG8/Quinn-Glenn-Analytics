Featured Project

NFL Draft Analytics: What Actually Predicts Success?

I combined college production, combine testing, and NFL outcomes for 292 wide receivers (2017–2025) to answer one question: Which pre-draft metrics actually separate stars from busts?

What I Did:
  • Built a SQLite database joining college stats, combine results, and NextGen Stats
  • Created a tiered classification system (Elite / Hit / Role / Bust) based on NFL production
  • Analyzed which metrics correlate with draft position vs. which predict actual success
What I Found:
  • Speed gets you drafted — 40-yard dash (r=0.38) correlates with draft position more than any college metric
  • YAC makes you a star — YAC above expected is the #1 separator: Elite (+1.10) vs. Bust (+0.33)
  • Round 2 is the value sweet spot — 78% success rate at a fraction of Round 1 cost
  • College TDs & yards per catch matter more than volume stats like YPG
